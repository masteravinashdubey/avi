I am a Research Scholar at IIT Bombay in Control & Computing group of Electrical Department. I am pursuing my PhD under Prof. Dwaipayan Mukherjee on Networked Dynamical Systems. I am also working on e-Yantra Project: A project in Computer Science department at IIT Bombay and supported by Ministry of Education (Formerly known as MHRD) through the National Mission on Education through ICT (NMEICT) to spread Embedded systems and Robotics education in colleges across India. I have worked on various project such as designing Self Balancing Robot using LQR control startegy and also via PID and comparing them. I have also worked on formation control problem using Spark V Robot and Firebird Robot.
